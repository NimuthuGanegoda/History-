# King Abhaya Naga

## Biography

<strong>Abhaya Naga</strong> (215–237 CE) – Abhaya Naga (215–237 CE) governed for 22 years, a duration permitting incremental infrastructural refinement. He authorized reservoir repairs, monastery endowments, and routine administration. Chronicles emphasize his piety and judicial fairness, sustaining the ideological synthesis of righteous kingship and Buddhist virtue.
