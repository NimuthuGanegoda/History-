# King Aggabodhi II

## Biography

<strong>Aggabodhi II</strong> (608–618 CE) – Aggabodhi II governed for ten years, sustaining hydraulic and religious infrastructure. His tenure involved reservoir upkeep, monastery sponsorship, and administrative routine. The era's relative stability enabled continuity of Anuradhapura's agrarian-monastic equilibrium.
