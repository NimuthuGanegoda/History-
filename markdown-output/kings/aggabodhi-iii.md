# King Aggabodhi III

## Biography

<strong>Aggabodhi III</strong> (626–636 CE) – Aggabodhi III ruled for ten years, sustaining the hydraulic-monastic synthesis. His tenure involved reservoir repairs, monastic endowments, and administrative routine. The era's stability enabled continuation of Anuradhapura's mature governance model.
