# King Aggabodhi IV

## Biography

<strong>Aggabodhi IV</strong> (658–674) – Aggabodhi IV ruled for 16 years, focusing on Buddhist scholarship and temple restoration. He maintained Anuradhapura's agricultural base through irrigation maintenance and navigated internal court politics. His reign represents steady governance amid the kingdom's ongoing challenges.
