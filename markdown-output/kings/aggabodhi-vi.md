# King Aggabodhi VI

## Biography

<strong>Aggabodhi VI</strong> (771–781 CE) – Aggabodhi VI governed for ten years, maintaining continuity in late 8th-century Anuradhapura. His tenure focused on irrigation maintenance, temple provisioning, and administrative routine. The era reflects sustained governance patterns despite broader regional shifts.
