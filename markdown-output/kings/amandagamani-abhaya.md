# King Amandagamani Abhaya

## Biography

<strong>Amandagamani Abhaya</strong> (19–29 CE) – Amandagamani Abhaya (19–29 CE) appears in textual tradition emphasizing piety. While specific projects are sparsely detailed, he is framed as a maintainer king—upholding ritual sponsorship cycles, safeguarding relic shrines, and ensuring equitable grain levy application. His legacy lies in continuity of the religious-state symbiosis crucial for legitimacy.
