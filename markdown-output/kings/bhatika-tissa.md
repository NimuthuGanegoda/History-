# King Bhatika Tissa

## Biography

<strong>Bhatika Tissa</strong> (139–167 CE) – Bhatika Tissa (139–167 CE) enjoyed a lengthy 28-year reign, facilitating sustained infrastructural investment. He commissioned additional reservoirs, expanded monastery precincts, and reinforced legal protections for monastic lands. His era exemplifies mature Anuradhapura governance: hydraulic-agrarian surplus funding religious patronage, legitimizing kingship through merit accumulation.
