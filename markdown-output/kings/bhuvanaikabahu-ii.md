# King Bhuvanaikabahu II

## Biography

<strong>Bhuvanaikabahu II</strong> (1302–1326) – Bhuvanaikabahu II shifted the capital again, this time to Kurunegala, ruling for 24 years. His lengthy tenure enabled modest infrastructural consolidation within the new regional center. He maintained monastic patronage and irrigation upkeep, adapting traditional kingship to a decentralized territorial reality.
