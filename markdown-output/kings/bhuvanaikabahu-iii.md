# King Bhuvanaikabahu III

## Biography

<strong>Bhuvanaikabahu III</strong> (1326–1335) – Bhuvanaikabahu III continued ruling from Kurunegala for nine years. He maintained temple restoration and irrigation upkeep, working within the constraints of a dispersed realm. His reign reflects steady adaptation to the ongoing fragmentation of Sinhalese political power.
