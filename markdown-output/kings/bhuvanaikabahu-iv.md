# King Bhuvanaikabahu IV

## Biography

<strong>Bhuvanaikabahu IV</strong> (1341–1351) – Bhuvanaikabahu IV founded the Gampola kingdom (near Kandy), ruling for ten years during the island's mid-14th-century fracture. He built new temples and irrigation in the highlands, inaugurating an era of regional courts. His reign exemplifies power's migration into defensible interior valleys.
