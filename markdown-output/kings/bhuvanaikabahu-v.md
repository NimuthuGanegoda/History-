# King Bhuvanaikabahu V

## Biography

<strong>Bhuvanaikabahu V</strong> (1372–1408) – Bhuvanaikabahu V's remarkable 36-year reign closed Gampola's era. He witnessed Kotte's rise and protected the Tooth Relic, ensuring Buddhism's continuity. His lengthy tenure provided stability amid regional competition, sustaining Gampola as a cultural beacon until succession shifts moved the center to Kotte.
