# King Bhuvanaikabahu VI

## Biography

<strong>Bhuvanaikabahu VI</strong> (1473–1480) – Bhuvanaikabahu VI ruled Kotte for seven years amid increasing rivalry among princes. He upheld monastic patronage and faced Portuguese merchant-explorers beginning to arrive on the coasts. His reign marks the earliest stirrings of European influence in the island's politics.
