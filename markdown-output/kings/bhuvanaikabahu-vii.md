# King Bhuvanaikabahu VII

## Biography

<strong>Bhuvanaikabahu VII</strong> (1521–1551) – Bhuvanaikabahu VII's 30-year reign at Kotte witnessed full-scale Portuguese involvement in island politics. He cultivated alliances with the Portuguese, granting them commercial privileges and a strategic fort at Colombo. His pragmatic diplomacy sought to balance power, but ultimately accelerated European colonization.
