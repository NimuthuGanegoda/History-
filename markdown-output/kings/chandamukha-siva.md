# King Chandamukha Siva

## Biography

<strong>Chandamukha Siva</strong> (43–52 CE) – Chandamukha Siva (43–52 CE) reigned during a quieter mid-1st century CE interval. Textual sources imply steady maintenance of Buddhist establishments and reservoir systems without major construction bursts. His rule exemplifies the administrative baseline—taxation, adjudication, ritual sponsorship—that sustained Anuradhapura's agrarian economy.
