# King Chulabhaya

## Biography

<strong>Chulabhaya</strong> (32–33 CE) – Chulabhaya (32–33 CE) had an almost token-length reign. The speed of succession indicates unresolved factional dynamics rather than systemic breakdown—agrarian and monastic institutions persisted. Historical treatment of his rule is an exercise in recognizing administrative inertia beyond royal personality.
