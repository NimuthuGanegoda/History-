# King Cula Naga

## Biography

<strong>Cula Naga</strong> (186–191 CE) – Cula Naga (186–191 CE) presided briefly, maintaining administrative continuity. His five-year tenure focused on routine governance: ensuring reservoir maintenance, collecting agrarian levies, and sponsoring monastic rituals. Absence of chronicler criticism implies effective if unremarkable stewardship.
