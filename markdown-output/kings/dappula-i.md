# King Dappula I

## Biography

<strong>Dappula I</strong> (804–835 CE) – Dappula I enjoyed a lengthy 31-year reign, facilitating sustained infrastructural investment. He commissioned reservoirs, restored stupas, and reinforced monastic endowments. His era represents stable early 9th-century governance, sustaining Anuradhapura's agrarian-monastic equilibrium through traditional legitimization mechanisms.
