# King Dappula IV

## Biography

<strong>Dappula IV</strong> (923–924 CE) – Dappula IV held a brief one-year reign. His tenure's brevity limited significant policy imprints; administrative continuity relied on entrenched bureaucratic networks. The rapid succession indicates ongoing elite contestation.
