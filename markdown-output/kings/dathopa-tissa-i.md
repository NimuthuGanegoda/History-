# King Dathopa Tissa I

## Biography

<strong>Dathopa Tissa I</strong> (614–623 CE) – Dathopa Tissa I governed for nine years, maintaining administrative and religious continuity. His reign focused on irrigation maintenance, temple grants, and routine adjudication. Chronicles emphasize piety and stability, reflecting effective governance in early 7th-century Anuradhapura.
