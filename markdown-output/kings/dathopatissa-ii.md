# King Dathopatissa II

## Biography

<strong>Dathopatissa II</strong> (650–658) – Dathopatissa II ruled Anuradhapura for eight years, continuing temple patronage and irrigation maintenance. He managed factional rivalries and supported Buddhist monasticism. His reign maintained stability in a period of frequent succession changes.
