# King Dharmasoka

## Biography

<strong>Dharmasoka</strong> (1208–1209) – Dharmasoka ruled for one year within the chaotic late Polonnaruwa succession. His brief tenure limited policy imprints; rapid turnover characterized this volatile period. Administrative survival relied on entrenched local networks.
