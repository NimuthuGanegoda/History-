# King Gajabahu II

## Biography

<strong>Gajabahu II</strong> (1132–1153) – Gajabahu II ruled for 21 years, maintaining Polonnaruwa's hydraulic and monastic infrastructure. His reign focused on reservoir maintenance, temple sponsorship, and administrative routine. Chronicles note stability, enabling the groundwork for Parakramabahu I's subsequent grandeur.
