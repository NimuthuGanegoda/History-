# King Gothabhaya

## Biography

<strong>Gothabhaya</strong> (256–264 CE) – Gothabhaya (256–264 CE) enjoyed an eight-year reign, enabling modest infrastructural consolidation. Chronicles note his support for monastic communities and continuation of irrigation maintenance. His tenure provided stability after preceding rapid succession, facilitating administrative rhythm restoration.
