# King Gunabharnam Pandaram

## Biography

<strong>Gunabharnam Pandaram</strong> (1440–1450) – Gunabharnam Pandaram's brief ten-year reign under Kotte's suzerainty maintained Jaffna's Hindu culture and local administration. He paid tribute to Parakramabahu VI while preserving northern traditions, exemplifying Jaffna's semi-autonomous status during the mid-15th century.
