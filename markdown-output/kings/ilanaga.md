# King Ilanaga

## Biography

<strong>Ilanaga</strong> (33–43 CE) – Ilanaga (33–43 CE) governed for a decade, allowing modest infrastructural reinforcement. Chronicles note his patronage of monastic communities and continuation of irrigation upkeep. His decade provided breathing space after the volatility marked by Queens Anula and Sivali, contributing to dynastic stabilization through moderate piety and administrative routine.
