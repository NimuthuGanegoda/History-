# King Jaya Bahu Pandaram

## Biography

<strong>Jaya Bahu Pandaram</strong> (1410–1440) – Jaya Bahu Pandaram ruled Jaffna for 30 years during Parakramabahu VI's unification campaigns. He resisted southern annexation and maintained Jaffna's Hindu traditions. Eventually, Kotte's military pressure forced his submission, ending Jaffna's full independence.
