# King Jaya Sinha

## Biography

<strong>Jaya Sinha</strong> (1323–1330) – Jaya Sinha ruled Jaffna for seven years, maintaining Hindu patronage and northern trade. His brief reign continued the dynasty's policies of temple-building and irrigation maintenance, sustaining Jaffna's maritime prosperity amid regional volatility.
