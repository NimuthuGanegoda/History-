# King Jayabahu I

## Biography

<strong>Jayabahu I</strong> (1110–1111) – Jayabahu I ruled briefly for one year after Vijayabahu I's lengthy tenure. His short reign limited significant policy imprints; succession dynamics likely dominated his brief rule. Administrative continuity relied on established bureaucratic networks.
