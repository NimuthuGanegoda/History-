# King Jettha Tissa III

## Biography

<strong>Jettha Tissa III</strong> (328–337 CE) – Jettha Tissa III (328–337 CE) ruled for nine years, sustaining the religious and hydraulic equilibrium established by Siri Meghavanna. His tenure focused on Tooth Relic veneration, monastic patronage, and irrigation upkeep. Chronicles emphasize continuity and piety, reflecting stable governance post-sectarian turbulence.
