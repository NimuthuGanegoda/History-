# Queen Kalyanavati

## Biography

<strong>Queen Kalyanavati</strong> (1202–1208) – Queen Kalyanavati governed for six years, one of the rare female rulers in Polonnaruwa. Her reign reflects succession turbulence following Nissanka Malla's lineage collapse. She maintained administrative continuity through court alliances, yet her tenure underscores the dynasty's fragility. Her rule illustrates female sovereignty emerging during male succession voids.
