# King Kanirajanu Tissa

## Biography

<strong>Kanirajanu Tissa</strong> (29–32 CE) – Kanirajanu Tissa (29–32 CE) ruled briefly; chronicles occasionally allude to severity in justice administration, though context is ambiguous and possibly moralizing. Structural impacts remain undocumented. His reign's abrupt end suggests contested acceptance among elites, feeding the pattern of intermittent short tenures.
