# King Kanittha Tissa

## Biography

<strong>Kanittha Tissa</strong> (167–186 CE) – Kanittha Tissa (167–186 CE) continued the pattern of tank construction and temple sponsorship established by predecessors. His 19-year reign enabled incremental expansion of irrigation networks and maintenance of existing stupas. Chronicles note his piety and adherence to Theravāda orthodoxy, sustaining the symbiotic state-Sangha relationship.
