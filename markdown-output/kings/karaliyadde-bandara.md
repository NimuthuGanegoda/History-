# King Karaliyadde Bandara

## Biography

<strong>Karaliyadde Bandara</strong> (1552–1581) – Karaliyadde Bandara's 29-year reign witnessed increasing Portuguese pressure on Kandy and the rise of Sitawaka. He preserved the Tooth Relic and Buddhist traditions amid regional warfare. Factional intrigue ended his reign, though the kingdom's independence survived.
