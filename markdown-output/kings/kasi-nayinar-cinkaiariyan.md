# King Kasi Nayinar Cinkaiariyan

## Biography

<strong>Kasi Nayinar Cinkaiariyan</strong> (1565–1570) – Kasi Nayinar Cinkaiariyan was installed by the Portuguese after his predecessor's overthrow, ruling Jaffna as a client for five years. He maintained Hindu ceremonies under Portuguese supervision, but his reign represented a sharp loss of autonomy for the northern kingdom.
