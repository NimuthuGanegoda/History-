# King Kassapa II

## Biography

<strong>Kassapa II</strong> (602–608 CE) – Kassapa II (602–608 CE) ruled for six years, maintaining continuity after Aggabodhi I's long tenure. His reign focused on routine governance: irrigation maintenance, temple endowments, and judicial administration. Chronicles note his piety, suggesting adherence to established Buddhist patronage norms.
