# King Kassapa III

## Biography

<strong>Kassapa III</strong> (732–738 CE) – Kassapa III governed for six years in the 8th century. His reign maintained irrigation networks and temple patronage amid increasing regional fragmentation pressures. Limited textual detail suggests routine governance focused on sustaining inherited infrastructural and religious frameworks.
