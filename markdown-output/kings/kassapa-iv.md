# King Kassapa IV

## Biography

<strong>Kassapa IV</strong> (898–914 CE) – Kassapa IV governed for sixteen years, enabling modest infrastructural investment. His tenure involved reservoir repairs, monastic sponsorship, and administrative routine. The era's stability facilitated continuation of Anuradhapura's agrarian-monastic equilibrium.
