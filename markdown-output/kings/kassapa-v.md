# King Kassapa V

## Biography

<strong>Kassapa V</strong> (914–923 CE) – Kassapa V ruled for nine years, sustaining hydraulic and religious infrastructure. His reign maintained irrigation networks, temple endowments, and judicial administration. Chronicles emphasize continuity and piety, reflecting effective routine governance.
