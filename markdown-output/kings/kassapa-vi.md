# King Kassapa VI

## Biography

<strong>Kassapa VI</strong> (924–935 CE) – Kassapa VI governed for eleven years, maintaining administrative and religious continuity. His reign focused on irrigation maintenance, temple patronage, and routine adjudication. The era reflects sustained governance patterns in early 10th-century Anuradhapura.
