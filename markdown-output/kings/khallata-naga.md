# King Khallata Naga

## Biography

<strong>King Khallata Naga</strong> (109–103 BCE) – Khallata Naga appears in chronicles with minimal elaboration, signifying a reign categorized by continuity rather than innovation. Operating within a matured hydraulic economy, he likely oversaw routine desilting cycles of reservoirs and reaffirmed monastery grants. Absence of polemical narrative indicates neither sectarian conflict nor foreign incursion dominated his years. His historical value lies in sustaining dynastic legitimacy through uneventful governance.
