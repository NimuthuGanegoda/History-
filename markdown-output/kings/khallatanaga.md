# King Khallatanaga

## Biography

<strong>Khallatanaga</strong> (109–103 BC) – Khallatanaga (Khallata Naga) was briefly mentioned in chronicles as a king during the troubled period after Lanja Tissa. His rule may have been concurrent or contested, reflecting the factional struggles of this era. Details of his reign remain sparse.
