# King Kirti Sri Rajasinha

## Biography

<strong>Kirti Sri Rajasinha</strong> (1747–1782) – Kirti Sri Rajasinha's 35-year reign witnessed a Buddhist renaissance; he invited Siamese monks to re-establish higher ordination and sponsored extensive temple construction. Despite Dutch coastal dominance, he preserved Kandy's cultural vibrancy and independence, making his era one of remarkable religious and artistic flourishing.
