# King Kuda Naga

## Biography

<strong>Kuda Naga</strong> (191–196 CE) – Kuda Naga (191–196 CE) similarly governed for five years without major transformative projects. His rule sustained the agrarian-monastic equilibrium, ensuring water distribution equity and temple provisioning. Historical interpretation centers on continuity and administrative functionality.
