# King Kulasekhara Cinkaiariyan

## Biography

<strong>Kulasekhara Cinkaiariyan</strong> (1330–1371) – Kulasekhara Cinkaiariyan ruled Jaffna for 41 years, a period of administrative consolidation and cultural flourishing. He sponsored Hindu temples, expanded maritime trade, and maintained autonomy from southern kingdoms. His long reign epitomizes Jaffna's golden age as a distinct Tamil polity.
