# King Kulasekhara

## Biography

<strong>Kulasekhara</strong> (1255–1284) – Kulasekhara ruled Jaffna for 29 years, consolidating the Tamil kingdom's administration. He built Hindu temples, managed trade with South India, and patronized Tamil literature. His reign strengthened Jaffna's identity as a distinct Hindu polity, setting it apart from the Buddhist kingdoms to the south.
