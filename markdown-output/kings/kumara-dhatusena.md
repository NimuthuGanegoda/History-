# King Kumara Dhatusena

## Biography

<strong>Kumara Dhatusena</strong> (512–524 CE) – Kumara Dhatusena (512–524 CE) ruled for twelve years, sustaining the post-Sigiriya restoration. His reign maintained irrigation networks, temple patronage, and administrative continuity. Chronicles emphasize piety and stability, framing his tenure as consolidation after the turbulent Kashyapa-Moggallana conflict.
