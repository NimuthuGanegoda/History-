# King Lokissara

## Biography

<strong>Lokissara</strong> (1210–1211) – Lokissara ruled for one year within the chaotic late Polonnaruwa sequence. His brief tenure reflects ongoing succession contestation. Limited textual evidence precludes detailed reconstruction; his role was primarily temporal bridging amid volatility.
