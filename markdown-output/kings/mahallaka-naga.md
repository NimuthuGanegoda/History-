# King Mahallaka Naga

## Biography

<strong>Mahallaka Naga</strong> (136–139 CE) – Mahallaka Naga (136–139 CE) had a short three-year tenure following Gajabahu I's dynamic rule. Limited textual detail suggests continuity governance: upholding irrigation cycles, temple grants, and routine administration. His reign functions as a bridging phase before Bhatika Tissa's longer consolidation.
