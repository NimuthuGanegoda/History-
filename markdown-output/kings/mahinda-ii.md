# King Mahinda II

## Biography

<strong>Mahinda II</strong> (781–797 CE) – Mahinda II ruled for sixteen years, enabling infrastructural consolidation. His reign involved reservoir repairs, monastic sponsorship, and routine administration. Chronicles emphasize stability and piety, sustaining the traditional state-Sangha synthesis.
