# King Mahinda III

## Biography

<strong>Mahinda III</strong> (801–804 CE) – Mahinda III ruled for three years, sustaining administrative and religious continuity. His brief tenure focused on irrigation upkeep and temple patronage. The era's rapid succession reflects ongoing elite dynamics in early 9th-century Anuradhapura.
