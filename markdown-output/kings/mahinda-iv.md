# King Mahinda IV

## Biography

<strong>Mahinda IV</strong> (956–972 CE) – Mahinda IV enjoyed a sixteen-year reign, enabling sustained infrastructural consolidation. His tenure involved reservoir repairs, monastic endowments, and administrative routine. Chronicles emphasize piety and stability, sustaining Anuradhapura's traditional governance model.
