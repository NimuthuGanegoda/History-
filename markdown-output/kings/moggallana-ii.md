# King Moggallana II

## Biography

<strong>Moggallana II</strong> (531–539) – Moggallana II ruled Anuradhapura for eight years, continuing monastic patronage and temple construction. He navigated ongoing sectarian debates among Buddhist schools and maintained irrigation systems. His reign stabilized the kingdom after earlier dynastic conflicts.
