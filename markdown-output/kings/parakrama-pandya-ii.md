# King Parakrama Pandya II

## Biography

<strong>Parakrama Pandya II</strong> (1212–1215) – Parakrama Pandya II ruled briefly before Kalinga Magha's devastating invasion. His three-year tenure marked Polonnaruwa's final gasp as a stable capital. Administrative effectiveness waned amid external threats and internal fragmentation, setting the stage for catastrophic foreign intervention.
