# King Parakramabahu II

## Biography

<strong>Parakramabahu II</strong> (1270–1284) – Parakramabahu II ruled from Dambadeniya for fourteen years, sustaining the restoration initiated by Vijayabahu III. He repelled South Indian incursions, patronized Buddhist scholarship, and maintained regional irrigation networks. His reign emphasized military defense and cultural revival, adapting to the post-Polonnaruwa fragmented landscape.
