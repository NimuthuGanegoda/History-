# King Parakramabahu III

## Biography

<strong>Parakramabahu III</strong> (1287–1293) – Parakramabahu III attempted a final stand at Polonnaruwa, reigning for six turbulent years. He restored monastic institutions and repaired irrigation, hoping to reclaim the city's glory. Yet mounting instability soon forced his successors to retreat southward, marking Polonnaruwa's twilight.
