# King Parakramabahu IV

## Biography

<strong>Parakramabahu IV</strong> (1291–1302) – Parakramabahu IV continued ruling from Yapahuwa for eleven years. His reign maintained Buddhist patronage and regional irrigation, navigating the challenges of a mobile, defensive kingship model. Limited grand construction reflects the era's focus on survival over expansion.
