# King Parakramabahu V

## Biography

<strong>Parakramabahu V</strong> (1344–1359) – Parakramabahu V's fifteen-year rule at Gampola advanced highland settlement and Buddhist culture. He managed relations with rival centers and continued monastic restoration. His tenure illustrates Sinhalese kingship's capacity to thrive despite territorial contraction.
