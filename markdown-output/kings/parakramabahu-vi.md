# King Parakramabahu VI

## Biography

<strong>Parakramabahu VI</strong> (1412–1467) – Parakramabahu VI, the last king to unify nearly the entire island, ruled from Kotte for an extraordinary 55 years. He championed Theravada Buddhism, sponsored grand temple construction, and oversaw a renaissance in Sinhalese art and literature. His reign stands as the final golden age before European colonialism.
