# King Parakramabahu VIII

## Biography

<strong>Parakramabahu VIII</strong> (1484–1508) – Parakramabahu VIII ruled Kotte for 24 years, a reign marked by Buddhist piety and growing Portuguese commercial influence. He promoted Theravada scholarship and maintained the Tooth Relic, yet could not prevent emerging European entanglements. His death ushered in a period of partition and intensified foreign pressure.
