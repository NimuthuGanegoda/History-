# King Periyapillai

## Biography

<strong>Periyapillai</strong> (1570–1582) – Periyapillai ruled Jaffna for 12 years under Portuguese suzerainty, managing a precarious autonomy. He paid tribute and allowed Portuguese garrisons, yet preserved Hindu worship and local Tamil administration. His reign exemplifies the northern kingdom's struggle to retain identity under colonial pressure.
