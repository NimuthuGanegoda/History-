# King Puviraja Pandaram III

## Biography

<strong>Puviraja Pandaram III</strong> (1582–1591) – Puviraja Pandaram III ruled Jaffna for nine years under tight Portuguese control. He navigated growing colonial demands while attempting to preserve Hindu religious institutions. His reign saw increasing Portuguese interference in local succession and administration.
