# King Rajadhi Rajasinha

## Biography

<strong>Rajadhi Rajasinha</strong> (1782–1798) – Rajadhi Rajasinha ruled Kandy for 16 years during the transition from Dutch to British colonial power. He maintained Buddhist rituals, faced internal court intrigue, and witnessed the British annexation of the Dutch territories. His reign marks the final generation before British conquest.
