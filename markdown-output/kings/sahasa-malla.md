# King Sahasa Malla

## Biography

<strong>Sahasa Malla</strong> (1200–1202) – Sahasa Malla ruled briefly for two years amid escalating succession conflicts. His tenure marked the onset of Polonnaruwa's rapid dynastic fragmentation. Limited administrative consolidation occurred; his reign functions as a transitional phase before the chaotic sequence involving queens and short-lived claimants.
