# King Sena I

## Biography

<strong>Sena I</strong> (833–853 CE) – Sena I governed for twenty years (note chronological overlap with Dappula II in sources, indicating possible co-regency or textual ambiguity). His lengthy tenure enabled hydraulic and monastic investment, sustaining irrigation networks and temple patronage. Chronicles emphasize stability and piety.
