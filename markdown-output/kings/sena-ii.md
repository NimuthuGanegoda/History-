# King Sena II

## Biography

<strong>Sena II</strong> (853–887 CE) – Sena II enjoyed a robust 34-year reign, one of the longest in late Anuradhapura history. His tenure facilitated sustained infrastructural consolidation: reservoir maintenance, monastic endowments, and administrative routine. His era represents mature 9th-century governance, balancing hydraulic stewardship with Buddhist patronage.
