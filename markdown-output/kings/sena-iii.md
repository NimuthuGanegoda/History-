# King Sena III

## Biography

<strong>Sena III</strong> (936–945 CE) – Sena III ruled for nine years, sustaining hydraulic and monastic infrastructure. His tenure involved reservoir upkeep, monastery sponsorship, and administrative routine. Chronicles note stability, continuing the traditional state-Sangha equilibrium.
