# King Silakala

## Biography

<strong>Silakala</strong> (518–531) – Silakala ruled Anuradhapura for 13 years after seizing the throne through political intrigue. He sponsored Buddhist monasteries and irrigation works, attempting to legitimize his reign through piety. His rule saw ongoing tensions between rival royal factions.
