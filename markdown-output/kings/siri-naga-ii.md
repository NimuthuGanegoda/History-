# King Siri Naga II

## Biography

<strong>Siri Naga II</strong> (237–245 CE) – Siri Naga II (237–245 CE) ruled for eight years, maintaining continuity after Abhaya Naga's extended tenure. His reign focused on sustaining irrigation networks and temple provisioning. Limited textual detail implies effective routine governance without dramatic reforms or crises.
