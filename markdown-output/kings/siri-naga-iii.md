# King Siri Naga III

## Biography

<strong>Siri Naga III</strong> (249–251 CE) – Siri Naga III (249–251 CE) ruled briefly after Abhaya Naga II's short tenure. His two-year reign likely focused on administrative stabilization following rapid succession. Limited inscriptional evidence suggests continuity governance: maintaining irrigation, temple grants, and routine adjudication.
