# King Siva II (second)

## Biography

<strong>Siva II</strong> (428–430 CE) – Siva II (428–430 CE) had a brief two-year reign amid escalating South Indian interference. His tenure marks the onset of a tumultuous period where Tamil invasions challenged Anuradhapura sovereignty. Limited inscriptional evidence suggests his rule ended violently, initiating a sequence of Tamil usurpers.
