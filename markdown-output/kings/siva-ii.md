# King Siva II

## Biography

<strong>Siva II</strong> (614–623) – Siva II ruled Anuradhapura for nine years during a period of complex dynastic politics. He maintained Buddhist patronage and irrigation, navigating factional disputes among rival claimants. His reign exemplifies the challenges of sustaining kingship amid frequent succession struggles.
