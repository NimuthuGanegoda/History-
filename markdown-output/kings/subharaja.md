# King Subharaja

## Biography

<strong>Subharaja</strong> (60–67 CE) – Subharaja (60–67 CE) ruled briefly without major inscriptional or architectural monuments. His reign likely focused on ensuring irrigation schedules, resolving land disputes, and honoring existing monastic endowments. Continuity over change characterizes his historical imprint.
