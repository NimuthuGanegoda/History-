# King Tissa

## Biography

<strong>Tissa</strong> (50–47 BC) – Tissa's brief three-year reign maintained Anuradhapura's Buddhist institutions. Limited information survives about his rule, suggesting either a peaceful interlude or a minor reign overshadowed by successors. He upheld monastic patronage during his short tenure.
