# King Udaya I

## Biography

<strong>Udaya I</strong> (887–898 CE) – Udaya I ruled for eleven years, maintaining continuity after Sena II's extended tenure. His reign focused on irrigation upkeep, temple provisioning, and routine administration. Chronicles note stability, sustaining the traditional state-Sangha synthesis.
