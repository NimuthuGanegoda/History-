# King Udaya II

## Biography

<strong>Udaya II</strong> (946–954 CE) – Udaya II governed for eight years, maintaining continuity in mid-10th-century Anuradhapura. His reign focused on irrigation maintenance, temple provisioning, and routine administration. Limited narrative detail implies effective governance without dramatic transformations.
