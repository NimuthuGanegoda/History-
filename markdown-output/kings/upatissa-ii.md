# King Upatissa II

## Biography

<strong>Upatissa II</strong> (525–526 CE) – Upatissa II held a one-year reign within a sequence of short tenures. His rule sustained routine governance: irrigation upkeep, temple provisioning, and adjudication. The era's rapid turnover underscores fragility in 6th-century dynastic succession.
