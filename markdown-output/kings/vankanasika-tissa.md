# King Vankanasika Tissa

## Biography

<strong>Vankanasika Tissa</strong> (111–114 CE) – Vankanasika Tissa (111–114 CE) followed Vasabha's infrastructure-heavy tenure with a short reign focused on maintaining new irrigation networks. His brief rule limited grand initiatives, yet he sustained the momentum of hydraulic stewardship, ensuring desilting and repair work continued.
