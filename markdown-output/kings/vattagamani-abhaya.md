# King Vattagamani Abhaya

## Biography

<strong>Vattagamani Abhaya</strong> (89–77 BC) – Vattagamani Abhaya's turbulent 12-year reign saw him driven into exile by Tamil invaders for 14 years before reclaiming the throne. He famously founded the Abhayagiri monastery, creating a rival sect to Mahavihara. His era marks a watershed in Theravada Buddhism's institutional development.
