# King Vijaya Kumara

## Biography

<strong>Vijaya Kumara</strong> (251–252 CE) – Vijaya Kumara (251–252 CE) held an ephemeral one-year reign. The speed of succession indicates either elite contestation or health-related turnover. His rule's brevity precluded significant policy imprints; administrative continuity relied on entrenched bureaucratic and monastic networks.
