# King Vijayabahu V

## Biography

<strong>Vijayabahu V</strong> (1335–1341) – Vijayabahu V ruled for six years from the Dambadeniya–Kurunegala region. His reign continued to support monastic life and maintain irrigation in the reduced heartland. Constant political fluidity limited architectural and administrative ambitions.
