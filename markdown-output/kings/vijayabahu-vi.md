# King Vijayabahu VI

## Biography

<strong>Vijayabahu VI</strong> (1513–1521) – Vijayabahu VI ruled a partitioned Kotte for eight years, competing with rival princes. He allowed Portuguese traders limited coastal footholds, a fateful concession. His reign saw the kingdom's influence wane as European interests grew and internal succession struggles intensified.
