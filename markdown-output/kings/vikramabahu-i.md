# King Vikramabahu I

## Biography

<strong>Vikramabahu I</strong> (1111–1132) – Vikramabahu I governed for 21 years, consolidating Polonnaruwa's infrastructure. His reign involved irrigation expansion, monastic patronage, and administrative stabilization. He navigated internal succession disputes and regional power balancing, sustaining the restored Sinhalese kingdom's fragile equilibrium.
