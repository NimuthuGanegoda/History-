# King Vikramabahu III

## Biography

<strong>Vikramabahu III</strong> (1357–1374) – Vikramabahu III's seventeen years at Gampola saw continued temple patronage and administrative stability. He navigated rivalry with emerging Kotte near Colombo, ensuring Gampola's cultural prominence. His reign shows how smaller kingdoms maintained Buddhist kingship ideals.
