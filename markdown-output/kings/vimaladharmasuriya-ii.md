# King Vimaladharmasuriya II

## Biography

<strong>Vimaladharmasuriya II</strong> (1687–1707) – Vimaladharmasuriya II ruled Kandy for 20 years after Rajasinha II. He navigated increasingly strained relations with the Dutch East India Company, preserved Buddhist traditions, and continued monastic patronage. His reign sustained Kandy's independence despite mounting European economic pressure.
