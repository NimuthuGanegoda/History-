# King Vira Narendra Sinha

## Biography

<strong>Vira Narendra Sinha</strong> (1707–1739) – Vira Narendra Sinha's 32-year reign saw intensified Dutch encroachment on Kandy's sovereignty. He patronized Buddhist arts, maintained the Tooth Relic's prestige, and managed court factionalism. His death ended the old Sinhalese dynasty, leading to the accession of a Nayakkar line from South India.
