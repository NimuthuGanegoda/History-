# King Yassalalaka Tissa

## Biography

<strong>Yassalalaka Tissa</strong> (52–60 CE) – Yassalalaka Tissa (52–60 CE) presided over a period of hydraulic refinement. Chronicles credit him with modest temple donations and reservoir repair. His tenure represents incremental reinforcement rather than dramatic innovation, upholding the ritual-economic equilibrium between palace and Sangha.
